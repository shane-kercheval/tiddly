Product Description

The Sony WH-1000XM5 represents the pinnacle of wireless noise-canceling headphone technology. With two processors controlling eight microphones, the Auto NC Optimizer automatically adjusts noise cancellation to your environment.

Key Features

  • Industry-Leading Noise Cancellation: Eight microphones and two processors work together to cancel more noise than ever before.
  • Exceptional Sound Quality: Newly designed 30mm drivers deliver crystal-clear audio with deep bass and crisp highs.
  • 30-Hour Battery Life: Listen all day with up to 30 hours of playback on a single charge. Quick charge provides 3 hours of playback from just 3 minutes of charging.
  • Premium Comfort: Lightweight design with soft-fit leather and pressure-relieving ear cushions for all-day comfort.
  • Multipoint Connection: Seamlessly switch between two Bluetooth devices.
  • Speak-to-Chat: Automatically pauses music when you start speaking.
